Project Management

Project Management Office (PM Office) is a web based tool that can be used to control the simple or complex projects. PM Office is about aligning people, projects and organizational priorities. This tool can helps the organizations in efficient and effective management of resources and both project-based and ongoing activities.

Contract Management

Contract Management System (CMS) is web enabled, customizable secure system to manage vendor relations. CMS provides a user friendly interface between a company and its vendor.It enables the company to quickly respond to fill in job requirement using vendor resources. The vendor can use this system to track the job requests and manage contracts.

Event Management

Event Management System is a web-based tool that facilitates online registration, payment, resource management, web-based accounting and reporting functionality. This web-enabled, customizable secure system ensure users authentication to access the system. The back office staff can take advantage of an intuitive transaction and reporting interface with a minimum of training.

Marketing Management

Marketing Management System is a web based Marketing Management system through which a company advertises it's available products. This tool helps the organizations in efficient and effective marketing of products and increasing it's sales. Therby increasing overall revenue.

Vendor Management

Vendor management system is Web-enabled, customizable secure system to manage vendor relations. It can provide you a single, unified view to understand how your company uses contractors and how much you are spending on them. It can also help you significantly shorten the time it takes to fill a request for a vendor resource.
