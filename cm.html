CMS

Contract Management System (CMS) is web enabled, customizable secure system to manage vendor relations. CMS provides a user friendly interface between a company and its vendor.It enables the company to quickly respond to fill in job requirement using vendor resources. The vendor can use this system to track the job requests and manage contracts.
